About

  • 2014-2018

    Humble Beginning

    In Secondary School I chose to be in photo, video and art classes. This was to feed my passion for art and design. During this time I created content for friends and online creators for a small charge.

  • 2020 - 2023

    College Begins

    Completed a full 3 Year Graphic Design program at Mohawk College in Hamilton, Ontario. Finishing in good standing and receiving a advanced diploma, I learned many new skills and techniques for use in the design industry.

  • 2023 - Present

    Strong Ambition

    Made the move from Ontario to Nova Scotia to begin a new chapter alongside my wife. Since then, I’ve been taking on small projects while steadily building a portfolio that reflects my passion and prepares me for the next step in my design career.

Bowling Team Logo

Personal Project

This was a small project that I began back in September 2024. Me and some friends of mine from work decided to join a bowling league at the local alley. Our captain came up with the name "Pinbusters" and had posted a simple image from google that he had found. So I took it upon myself to sketch and create us a custom logo which we now wear proudly.

  • Client: Digby Bowling Team
  • Category: Brand Design

Magazine Ad

College Program Project

This was a college assignment where the fake client is a shoe company called Strike that is looking to advertise their brand new shoe called the Strike TurboCharge 4. The client added that they had requirements that must be met for each ad and to include specific logos and website information. My design is a eye catching magazine ad that uses contrast and hierarchy to grab the readers attention. This project was completed using the Adobe InDesign Program.

  • Client: Mohawk College
  • Category: Layout Design

Resume Brochure

I had the opportunity to ask and find out exactly what the client wanted and was given information to complete the entire copy. The student was left satisfied with the result and gave me great feedback in the end.

  • Client: College Student
  • Category: Print Design

Organization Logo

College Program Project

A new Mexican restaurant in Hamilton called "ROJO" need a new menu design to reflect their contemporary dining experience. Using modern and simple visuals and layout, we were able to design a multi-page menu that features all dishes, drinks and sides including attention for the Fajitas that chef wanted us to include. This project was done using the Adobe InDesign Program.

  • Client: Caledona Regional Chamber of Commerce
  • Category: Brand Design

Business Rebrand

College Program Project

A small local business wanted to have a full rebrand. We were a team of 5, and were all tasked with individual jobs for this assignemnt. I was able to pitch many new ideas and concepts for the new logo redesign and colour theme. I was also tasked with completing most of the Brand Guideline booklet which is previewed on my site. We had a meeting with the client and presented our proposal, they were very impressed with the work completed.

  • Client: Brantford Finder's Keepers
  • Category: Packaging Design

Posters

College Program Project

Our objective with this project was to utilize our typography knowledge to create a logotype design for multiple posters. We were given the ability to create a logo for a fictional client, I ended up brainstorming and creating a fake band called "Tear of Fears." I came up with multiple concepts and found a design that I was able to utilize a theme out of for the posters. Using contrast and visuals, I was able to effectively pop the text off the page to bring to the viewers' attention. All information was presented well, and each poster had the bands vector details integrated into every design.

  • Client: Mohawk College
  • Category: Layout Design
