About Me

Hello, thank you for visiting my portfolio!

I’m a Graphic Designer with a passion for creating compelling visuals that bring businesses, creators and ideas to life. Specializing in brand design, illustration, web development, and print media.

My journey started with a love for visual storytelling. I worked with content creators and individuals, designing assets for their websites and social media. This love for digital design led me to complete a three-year Graphic Design program, where I mastered industry-standard tools like Adobe Creative Suite and collaborated on real-world projects with agencies and businesses.

Image Editing

Improve, correct, or transform photos to meet your needs. This includes color adjustments, retouching, background removal, and preparing images for both print and digital use.

Brand Design

Print Design

Bowling Team Logo

Personal Project

This was a small project that I began back in September 2024. Some friends from work and myself decided to join a bowling league at the local alley. Our captain came up with the name "Pinbusters" and had posted a simple image from google that he had found. So I took it upon myself to sketch and create us a custom logo which we now wear proudly.

  • Client: Digby Bowling Team
  • Category: Brand Design

Barbershop Sign

Work Project

This project involved the design and layout of a custom A-frame sign for a local barbershop in Annapolis Royal. The goal was to create a bold, eye-catching piece that would attract foot traffic and clearly communicate the business from the street level. Strong typography, simple graphics, and high contrast were used to ensure maximum readability in all lighting conditions.

  • Client: Beau The Barber
  • Category: Print Design

Organization Logo

College Program Project

A local organization came to the college to give us all the opportunity to pitch new ideas for a redesign of their logo. Each student had the chance to jot down details during a meeting with the client to understand the projects values and details they wanted us to include. We were put into groups of 4, each of us bringing our own concepts to the table. We ended up choosing my concept to finalize and present to the client. We had a meeting with the client and they were impressed with the logo presented.

  • Client: Caledona Regional Chamber of Commerce
  • Category: Brand Design
