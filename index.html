Organization Logo

College Program Project

A local organization came to the college to give us all the opportunity to pitch new ideas for a redesign of their logo. Each student had the chance to jot down details during a meeting with the client to understand the projects values and details they wanted us to include. We were put into groups of 4, each of us bringing our own concepts to the table. We ended up choosing my concept to finalize and present to the client. We had a meeting with the client and they were impressed with the logo presented.

  • Client: Caledona Regional Chamber of Commerce
  • Category: Brand Design

Posters

College Program Project

Our objective with this project was to utilize our typography knowledge to create a logotype design for multiple posters. We were given the ability to create a logo for a fictional client, I ended up brainstorming and creating a mockup band called "Tear of Fears." I came up with multiple concepts and found a design that I was able to utilize a theme out of for the posters. Using contrast and visuals, I was able to effectively pop the text off the page to bring to the viewers' attention. All information was presented well, and each poster had the bands vector details integrated into every design.

  • Client: Mohawk College
  • Category: Print Design
